About Us

Welcome to INSPIRED ACADEMY – a trusted tuition and learning center dedicated to the academic success of students from Class 1 to 12.

We provide high-quality coaching in all major subjects including Maths, Science, English, Social Science, Hindi, Accountancy, Economics, and more. Our team of experienced teachers ensures that every student gets personalized attention and learns in a comfortable, focused environment.

Whether your child needs help catching up, preparing for exams, or staying ahead in class – INSPIRED ACADEMY is the perfect place for academic support, confidence building, and overall growth.

Guiding Students Towards Academic Success

At INSPIRED ACADEMY, we provide quality coaching for students of Class 1 to 12 with a focus on understanding, revision, and results.

  • Subject-wise tuition for all boards – Maths, Science, English, SST, Hindi & more.
